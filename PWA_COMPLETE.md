## 📱 Installation Instructions

### 🍎 iPhone/iPad (Safari)
1. **Open Safari** → Go to your website
2. **Tap Share** button (square with arrow)
3. **Scroll down** → "Add to Home Screen"
4. **Tap "Add"** → App appears on home screen
5. **Tap icon** → Opens in standalone mode

### 🤖 Android (Chrome)
1. **Open Chrome** → Go to your website  
2. **Look for icon** ⬇ in address bar
3. **Tap "Install app"** → Confirmation dialog
4. **Tap "Install"** → App in app drawer
5. **Launch from home** → Full screen experience
